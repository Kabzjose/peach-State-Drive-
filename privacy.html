Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

At Peach State Drive Rentals ("we," "our," or "us"), we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or rent a vehicle from us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you make a reservation, sign up for our newsletter, or contact customer support. This information may include:

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Documentation: Driver’s license number, expiration date, and date of birth (for identity and age verification).
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details and billing address. (Note: Payment data is processed securely by third-party payment processors).
  • Vehicle Data: If our vehicles are equipped with GPS or telematics systems, we may collect location data, mileage, and fuel levels for fleet management and safety purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide a seamless rental experience. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Process and confirm your vehicle reservation.
  • Verify your identity and eligibility to rent (e.g., age and license validity).
  • Communicate with you regarding your booking, updates, or customer service inquiries.
  • Send you marketing communications, such as special offers or newsletters (you may opt out at any time).
  • Improve our website functionality and customer service.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, insurance verification, and email delivery.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).
  • Safety & Protection: We may disclose information to protect the rights, property, or safety of Peach State Drive Rentals, our customers, or others.

4. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and tracking technologies to customize our website and improve your experience. Most browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can remove or reject cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect the availability and functionality of the website.

6. Third-Party Websites

The website may contain links to third-party websites and applications of interest. Once you use these links to leave our website, any information you provide to these third parties is not covered by this Privacy Policy, and we cannot guarantee the safety and privacy of your information.
